About

My practice focuses on understanding and critiquing the impacts of emerging technologies on people, culture, and the planet. I work collectively to rethink the narratives and behaviours surrounding technologies — prioritising collaborative research as a process for testing, imagining and prototyping more equitable, alternative futures.

Associate lecturer at UAL (LCF + CCI). Selected clients: Neu Futur, DAZED, co—matter, MØRNING, WeTransfer, Protein, Midnight Agency, Softer, Selfridges.
